Best Study Spaces on Campus

Finding the perfect study space on campus isn't always easy. Here are my favorite go-to spots and hidden gems I found while exploring!

1. Library

The library is a great place to study, whether you're on your own or with others. You can books study rooms too if you need space to yourself or just want a space for you and your study group to work on something.

2. Office Hours

Go to office hours!!! It helps a lot! Not only can you ask the profssor and/or TA for help as you work, but you can also work with other students and bond over the struggle

3. Tutoring Center

If it's not time for office hours, the tutoring center is also a great place. The student tutors there may have taken the same class you did in the past and can help. You can also organize group tutoring sessions for a particular subject.

4. Cafe/Food courts

You can grab a bite to eat nearby if you get hungry while studying! Bonus if the weather is good enough for you to also lay down on a blanket in the grass
